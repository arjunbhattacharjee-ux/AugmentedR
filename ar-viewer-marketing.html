How it works

Three steps,
zero friction

01
Upload your image
Tap the upload zone or drag any image — PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP. Or pick one of our samples.
02
Allow camera access
One tap grants camera access. Works on any phone or desktop. HTTPS is handled automatically on Vercel.
03
Place it in your world
Drag to reposition. Pinch or scroll to resize. Your image floats live over your camera feed.
